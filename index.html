ABOUT

Audela Technologies is a boutique software development company with focus on quality and customer satisfaction.

We are based in India, incubated at KITCO, an organization that has provided consultancy services for more than 600 projects across the Nation as well as outside the country. The projects include airports, large industrial plants, industrial parks, special economic zones, hotels, resorts, roads and bridges. KTICO is the key mentoring organization behind Audela Technologies providing support in management and engineering areas.
